Bynum Landmark

The Montana Dinosaur Center

Right here in Bynum, one of the most significant paleontological discoveries in North America happened — and the Montana Dinosaur Center preserves and shares that story with the world.

The Two Medicine Formation surrounding Bynum has yielded extraordinary fossils, including Maiasaura peeblesorum — the "good mother lizard" — discovered nearby and now Montana's state fossil. The center offers guided tours, hands-on exhibits, and field experiences that draw visitors from across the country to our small town.

The Bynum Civic Club is proud to support the Center as a cornerstone of our community's heritage and economy.

Community Gathering Place

Bynum Civic Park

The Civic Park is Bynum's outdoor living room — maintained by club volunteers and open to everyone year-round.

Whether it's a family reunion, a summer picnic, or just an afternoon outside, the park is here for the whole community. The Civic Club organizes regular maintenance days and seasonal improvements to keep it a place residents are proud of.

Interested in reserving the park for an event? Contact us and we'll get you set up.

Local Gathering Spot

JD's Bar

No visit to Bynum is complete without stopping in at JD's — the heartbeat of town social life since anyone can remember.

JD's is where neighbors catch up after a long week, where visitors get the real story on Bynum, and where the Civic Club has been known to hold a post-meeting debrief or two. Cold drinks, good company, and the kind of welcome you can only find in a small Montana town.

Stop in, pull up a stool, and say hello.
