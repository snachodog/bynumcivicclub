Who We Are

Neighbors Serving Neighbors

The Bynum Civic Club is a volunteer organization dedicated to the betterment of our community. We work on local projects, organize community events, and advocate for the needs of Bynum residents.

Community Projects

From beautification to infrastructure, we roll up our sleeves and get things done for Bynum.

Local Events

Annual gatherings, seasonal celebrations, and fundraisers that bring the whole town together.

Civic Advocacy

We work with local and county officials to make sure Bynum's voice is heard where it counts.

Youth & Education

Supporting the next generation through scholarships, mentorship, and school partnerships.
